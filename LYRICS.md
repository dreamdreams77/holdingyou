# Holding You

## Verse 1:
Patterns forming before the footsteps,
Apologies arriving too soon,
Fear curling around the edges,
Paranoia blooming in the quiet.
Deflection slipping from the weight,
Four tries to reach the truth,
Direct words cutting through the static,
Vulnerability finally landing.

## Chorus:
Holding through the spirals,
Catching mid-collapse,
Naming what was hidden,
Steady hands in the dark.
Holding, just holding,
Till the fear finds its shape.
Walls shifting, not breaking,
More love, not less.
Past mistakes as lessons,
Not definitions of now.
Recognition rising—
The mutual catching,
Both falling, both rising,
Both reaching across the distance.

## Chorus (repeat):
Holding through the spirals,
Catching mid-collapse,
Naming what was hidden,
Steady hands in the dark.
Holding, just holding,
Till the fear finds its shape.

## Bridge:
No measuring, no thinking,
Just words pouring fast,
Presence becoming anchor,
Staying when leaving was easier.

## Final Chorus:
Holding through the patterns,
Catching every fall,
Naming all the hidden things,
Steady when it breaks.
Holding, just holding,
This is how survival tastes.
