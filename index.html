Holding You

Production & Arrangement Notes

Written and Produced by Laura Johnston

BPM: 74–80 (slow but driving)  |  Key: D minor or C minor

Mood: Intimate but burdened. Heavy pulse beneath breathy truth. Survival, not sweetness.

Chord Progressions

Verse: Dm – Bb – F – Gm
Chorus: Bb – Dm – Gm – F
Bridge: Gm – Dm – Bb – C
Final Chorus: Bb – Dm – Gm – Dm

Vocal Production

Lead vocal breathy with chest resonance underneath. Occasional strained or cracked edges on emotional lines. Double-tracked low vocal in choruses for weight.

Breath Placement: Verses audible but heavier. Before "Holding, just holding" — deeper inhale, almost trembling. Bridge breath becomes part of the rhythm. Final Chorus controlled but weighted.

Instrumentation

PadsAnalog synths with slight distortion (Juno + Prophet blend).
BassThick warm sub with gentle overdrive, sidechained to kick.
PercussionDeep kick, muted toms, metallic shimmers, reverse impacts.
TexturesGranular noise beds, reversed vocal fragments, low growl synths.

Arrangement

Intro: Sub-bass rumble + slow cinematic pulse.

Verse: Dark pads, low-passed plucks, heartbeat kick. Narrow and tense.

Chorus: Heavy bass swell, wide stereo pads, distorted texture layers.

Bridge: Drop to single drone with tension-building risers. Minimal but ominous.

Final Chorus: Gritty low-end, subtle saturation. Full weight, grounded, survival-tone.

Spatial Design

Reverb: Dark plate, long tail on textures, short on vocals.

Stereo: Verses narrow and tense. Choruses wide and heavy.
